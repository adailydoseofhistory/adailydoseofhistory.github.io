About A Daily Dose of Quiz

A Daily Dose of Quiz is a free trivia website that publishes a fresh batch of multiple-choice questions every day, spanning history, science, sports, entertainment, current affairs, and more.

Every quiz is reviewed and added by the editor before it goes live, so the questions on this site stay consistent and accurate — nothing here is generated or edited by visitors.

Why we built this

A few minutes of trivia is a simple, free way to learn something new and keep your general knowledge sharp. Our goal is to make that a daily habit — one short dose at a time.
